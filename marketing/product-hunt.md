## Description

**Departure is a fitness RPG built for people who actually go to the gym.**

Most fitness apps are either serious trackers that are boring, or gamified apps that don't track real workouts. We built both.

**How it works:**
- Log your actual training — sets, reps, weight across 88+ exercises
- Every workout earns XP based on real effort (not "do 50 pushups")
- Progress through 24 rank tiers from Recruit to Legend
- Form a team and battle through 24 monsters on an epic journey map
- Collect equipment drops with 5 rarity tiers
- Our anti-cheat system keeps progression honest — no fake Legends

**What makes it different:**
- AI Future Self: Upload a selfie, set your goals. AI generates what you could realistically look like
- Team battles: Your workouts fuel your team's combat power against monsters
- Dark Souls aesthetic: Built for adults. No anime, no cartoon characters
- Anti-cheat: Weight limits, spike detection, daily caps — every rule is transparent
- Free to play: No subscriptions, no ads, no paywall on core features

**Tech stack:** React Native (Expo SDK 54), Supabase, Google Gemini AI, RevenueCat

Built by Qualia AI. Free on iOS and Android.

## Maker Comment

Hey Product Hunt! I'm Kyle, and I built Departure because I was tired of choosing between a good workout tracker and a fun fitness app.

I've been lifting for years and tried every tracker out there. The serious ones (Strong, JEFIT) are great but have zero motivation built in. The gamified ones (LevelUp, Arise) are fun but don't actually track real workouts with sets, reps, and weight.

Departure does both. It's a full gym-grade workout tracker wrapped in a Dark Souls-inspired RPG. Your bench press doesn't just build your chest — it builds your Strength stat and helps your team kill the Kraken.

Some things I'm proud of:
- The anti-cheat system (6 layers of fraud detection — honest effort only)
- AI Future Self (Gemini generates what you could realistically look like)
- 24-monster team journey map (your workouts fuel team combat power)
- 150+ hand-generated art assets (monsters, warriors, equipment, banners)

It's 100% free to play. Premium packs exist but they're one-time purchases — no subscriptions.
