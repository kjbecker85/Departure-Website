## Full Description

Turn every workout into an epic quest. Departure tracks your real sets, reps, and weight — then rewards you with XP, equipment drops, and team monster battles.

Not a game pretending to be fitness. A real workout tracker with RPG progression that makes consistency addicting.

<b>LOG REAL WORKOUTS</b>
Track sets, reps, and weight across 88+ exercises in 10 categories — Arms, Chest, Back, Shoulders, Legs, Core, Cardio, Power, Stamina, and Flex. Schedule your training with 9 different methods including Push/Pull/Legs, Upper/Lower, Full Body, HIIT, and Custom.

<b>EARN XP & RANK UP</b>
Every exercise earns XP based on your actual effort — not arbitrary quests. Progress through 24 rank tiers from Recruit to Legend. Four warrior stats (STR, DEF, RES, END) reflect your real training balance.

<b>AUTO-DETECT PERSONAL RECORDS</b>
PRs are tracked automatically using the Epley 1RM formula for strength and duration for cardio. Just train and get notified when you set a new record.

<b>FIGHT MONSTERS WITH YOUR TEAM</b>
Form a team and battle through 24 monsters on an epic journey map — from Goblin Scouts to the Celestial Arbiter. Six themed worlds. Every workout contributes 10% of your XP to your team's combat power.

<b>COLLECT EQUIPMENT & LOOT</b>
Earn equipment drops from battles across 5 rarity tiers. Equip gear to boost your warrior stats and team lethality. Choose from 18 team genres — Barbarian, Cyberpunk, Samurai, and more.

<b>AI-POWERED FUTURE SELF</b>
Upload a selfie and set your goals. Our AI generates a realistic image of what you could look like — personalized to your body, nutrition, and commitment level.

<b>DAILY & WEEKLY CHALLENGES</b>
Fresh challenges every day and week. Complete them for bonus XP, coins, and temporary combat buffs.

<b>ANTI-CHEAT BUILT IN</b>
6-layer fraud detection ensures honest progression. Every rule is transparent. Honest effort only.

<b>FREE TO PLAY</b>
No subscriptions. No ads. No paywall on core features. Premium packs are optional one-time purchases.
