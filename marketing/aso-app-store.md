## Description

Turn every workout into an epic quest. Departure tracks your real sets, reps, and weight — then rewards you with XP, equipment drops, and team monster battles.

Not a game pretending to be fitness. A real workout tracker with RPG progression that makes consistency addicting.

LOG REAL WORKOUTS
Track sets, reps, and weight across 88+ exercises in 10 categories — Arms, Chest, Back, Shoulders, Legs, Core, Cardio, Power, Stamina, and Flex. Schedule your training with 9 different methods including Push/Pull/Legs, Upper/Lower, Full Body, HIIT, and Custom.

EARN XP & RANK UP
Every exercise earns XP based on your actual effort — not arbitrary quests. Progress through 24 rank tiers from Recruit to Legend. Four warrior stats (STR, DEF, RES, END) reflect your real training balance.

AUTO-DETECT PERSONAL RECORDS
PRs are tracked automatically using the Epley 1RM formula for strength exercises and duration for cardio. No manual logging needed — just train and get notified when you set a new record.

FIGHT MONSTERS WITH YOUR TEAM
Form a team and battle through 24 monsters on an epic journey map — from Goblin Scouts to the Celestial Arbiter. Six themed worlds: Goblin Horde, Undead Legion, Orc Warband, Ancient Beasts, Dragon Flight, and Titan Lords. Every workout contributes 10% of your XP to your team's combat power.

COLLECT EQUIPMENT & LOOT
Earn equipment drops from battles across 5 rarity tiers. Equip gear to boost your warrior stats and team lethality. Choose from 18 team genres — Barbarian, Cyberpunk, Samurai, and more — each with unique banner art.

AI-POWERED FUTURE SELF
Upload a selfie and set your goals. Our AI generates a realistic image of what you could look like — personalized to your body type, nutrition, and commitment level. It's not a filter. It's a target.

DAILY & WEEKLY CHALLENGES
Fresh challenges every day and week tied to your stat categories. Complete them for bonus XP, coins, and temporary combat buffs.

ANTI-CHEAT BUILT IN
6-layer fraud detection ensures honest progression: weight limits, spike detection, daily caps, and more. Every rule is transparent — check the Scoring Info page anytime. We'd rather have honest Recruits than fake Legends.

FREE TO PLAY
No subscriptions. No ads. No paywall on core features. Premium packs are optional one-time purchases that accelerate your journey — not gate it.
