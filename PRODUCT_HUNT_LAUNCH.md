# Product Hunt Launch — Departure: Fitness Quest

## Tagline (60 char max)
Dark Souls meets fitness — a workout RPG for adults

## Description
Departure turns your real workouts into RPG progression. Log actual exercises with sets, reps, and weight. Earn XP, rank up through 24 tiers, collect equipment, and battle 24 monsters with your team. Upload a selfie and AI generates your future transformation. Free to play, no paywall on core features. Built for adults who lift, not kids who tap.

## Maker Comment
I built Departure because every fitness RPG I tried felt like it was made for kids. Retro pixel art, anime themes, aggressive paywalls. I wanted something that looked like Dark Souls, tracked real workouts (not "do 50 pushups"), and made me actually want to open the app after leg day. The AI future-self feature was the hook that got my testers addicted — seeing what you could look like in 90 days is a different kind of motivation. The whole app is free to use. Paying just gets you cosmetics and premium equipment.
